EXPLORE-SRILANKA

Sri Lanka is a tropical island in the Indian Ocean, known for its golden beaches, lush tea plantations, ancient temples, and vibrant wildlife. Tourists can explore UNESCO World Heritage sites like Sigiriya Rock Fortress and the Sacred City of Kandy, enjoy safaris in Yala National Park to spot elephants and leopards, or take scenic train rides through misty hill country. With its mix of Buddhist culture, colorful festivals, and warm hospitality, Sri Lanka offers a compact yet diverse travel experience where beaches, mountains, and heritage sites are all within easy reach.
